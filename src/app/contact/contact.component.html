SCOTT MORRISON Australia Prime Minister

Scott John Morrison (born 13 May 1968) is an Australian politician who is the 30th and current prime minister of Australia. He took office in August 2018 upon his election as leader of the Liberal Party, and subsequently led the Coalition to victory at the 2019 federal election.

VLADIMIR PUTIN Russian President

The president of the Russian Federation (Russian: Президе́нт Росси́йской Федера́ции, tr. Prezident Rossiyskoy Federatsii), is the head of state of the Russian Federation, as well as the commander-in-chief of the Russian Armed Forces. It is the highest office in Russia.
